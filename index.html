About Us

Welcome to Omen Spark, an exhilarating one-month initiative by HP's Omen laptop line. Taking place in four vibrant cities at our Omen Playground stores, Omen Spark features specially curated events for tech enthusiasts, gamers, and aspiring professionals. Experience cutting-edge Omen laptops, participate in thrilling competitions, and explore HP's expertly designed courses to level up your career. Join us to connect with like-minded individuals, learn from industry experts, and elevate your tech and gaming skills. Discover, learn, and excel with Omen Spark!
